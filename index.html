About Me

I took a vaction to Puerto Rico. During, my vaction I got to tour a naval fort that protected puerto rico. The best part about the naval fort was the view of the ocean. Also, I was impressed by the size of the fort.

On my vaction to Puerto Rico, I got to tour a the Bacardi distillery. Bacardi was founded in Cuba, but made it way into Puerto Rico. On the tour I got to see the oak barrel that rum distilled in. Plus, I got to try a couple samples of the best Barcadi Rum.
